About Me



I’m not your typical entry-level candidate.

Over 12 years ago, I graduated from a technical high school with a focus on networking and telecommunication. Life took me in a different direction — My passion for technology, hacking, and computers remained strong at all times. Like many others, I grew up fascinated by the idea of becoming “a hacker” one day.

After two failed attempts to dive into cybersecurity — one during school time (with limited access to resources), and another when I was going through financially tough times — I finally decided to go all in. I organized my life, fixed my finances, and made a promise to myself: Now or never.

I studied daily while working 50–60 hours a week abroad, spending my weekends and evenings learning cybersecurity, talking to professionals, and dissecting every concept I could find. I passed my CompTIA Security+ exam and started building a practical skill set through labs, projects, and OSINT work.

What sets me apart isn’t speed — it’s consistency. I’m disciplined, relentless, and willing to make sacrifices. Years ago, I transformed my body through sport and became a certified personal trainer. Today, I’m doing the same with my career — building it from scratch, brick by brick.

Right now, my goal is clear: find my first role in IT or cybersecurity, return to Poland, and fully dedicate my time to growing in this field — immersing myself in the field professionally. I may not be the best yet, but I’m the kind of a person who will earn your trust over time.

If that remainds you of a person who you’d want to join your team — feel free to contact me.
